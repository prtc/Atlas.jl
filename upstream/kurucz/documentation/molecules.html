Kurucz/Molecules


 
*** See /LINELISTS for atomic and molecular linelists sorted by wavelength.
 
This is a list of molecules for which I have directories and plan to produce
new or improved linelists.  The web directories are to provide the details
of that work.  Much improved laboratory data are available for the isotopomers
of many of the molecules listed in /LINELISTS/LINESMOL and work on some of
them has been started.  However, I have been concentrating on improving the
atomic data before turning to the molecules.  I am willing to raise the
priority of an individual molecule if there is demand for it.  Linelists for
triatomic molecules have been produced by other researchers and have been
reformatted for ease of use.  Other polyatomic molecules will be added as
data become available.
 
This version of RMOLECASC.FOR will read all the *.asc files here.
 
(When I started this work + and - were not allowed in file names.)
