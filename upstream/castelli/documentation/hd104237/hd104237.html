Herbig Ae star-Spectroscopic binary.  A and B are the primary and the secondary stars.


A star parameters : Teff=8000 K, logg=3.7, vturb=2.3 km/sec, vsini=0 km/sec, vmacro=13 km/sec
B star parameters : Teff=5000 K, logg=3.7, vturb=1.0 km/sec (adopted), vsini=12 km/sec 
 
The A star parameters were determined from:
Fe I-Fe II ionization equilibrium, and the constraints of a  mass ratio M(A)/M(B)=1.29 (Boehm et al.,2004)
and a gravity logg=3.7 for the secondary. 

The B star parameters were determined from the Ca I profile at 6122.21 A (the gravity) and the
constraints of a luminosity ratio L(A)/L(B)=8.32 (Boehm et al. 2004) togheter with Teff=8000 K for
the A star.  

The corresponding radii ratio is [R(B)/R(A)]^2=0.77
