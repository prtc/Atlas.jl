ACS colors from ODFNEW ATLAS9 fluxes

In each grid there are two different columns of bolometric corrections BCv.
The BCv07 column lists BCv's normalized to BCv(Sun)=-0.07mag.
The BCv201 column lists BCv's normalized by setting to zero the smallest 
(in absolute value) BCv of the whole synthetic grid computed for [M/H]=0.0.
With this second normalization BCv(Sun)=-0.201 mag.
The two sets of BCv differ for a constant equal to 0.131 mag.
